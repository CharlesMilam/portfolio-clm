About...

I am a recent graduate of MakerSquare, and currently doing contract web development/admin work for a variety of small businesses. Most recently I was a paramedic, spent 7 years as a stay at home dad raising my two girls, and prior to that, worked for 10+ years at Applied Materials. At Applied I created a multitude of custom MS Access database applications, making extensive use of Visual Basic for Applications, in support of the materials and logistics groups of several divisions. I am looking to leverage my broad experience set into a junior web development role.
